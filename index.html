About Me

Durham 2015: Studying Front-End Engineering at The Iron Yard was an awesome experience. For 12 intense weeks I spent 70-80 hours a week studying HTML5, CSS3, & JavaScript. The process of web development fascinates me in an entirely new way. It intrigues me that so much depends on zeros & ones, that we've built a culture of technology that has shifted the way we communicate, & that to write code, one must learn how a computer processes information. I am excited about how much there is to learn, while striving to strike a balance between using logic with creativity in my career. It's an incredible process to build something that joins people all over the world.
